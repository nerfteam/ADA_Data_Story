Matching

A match made in heaven...

Our assumption is that during the period of the political campaigns (treatment), politicians get more media attention, i.e. they are more cited, and therefore have more quotes regarding climate change (outcome).

How could we test this? Well, simply we will observe number of quotes speaker had in the periods of time when campaings were actual, and when they were not. And, the most interesting part, we will match speakers with...themselves! So, we will observe how many quotes one had in the period without campaigns (i.e. without treatment), and in the period during the campaings (i.e. with treatment). And what about confounders? Well, we are matching them with themselves, perfect match, same characteristics, no confounders! Based on the informations provided in the article, we will use as campaing period the period of year and a half before the elections.
